ourselfRoles and Responsibility of Accountant
Responsible for managing client financial communications, processing invoices and
payments, maintaining accurate financial records, and coordinating closely with the
Finance Officer and Warehouse team to ensure smooth financial operations and product
release.
Key Responsibilities:
1. Client Communication (Post-Arrival & Dispatch):
Shipment Arrival Notification: Contact clients upon shipment arrival at the warehouse
to inform them that products have been received and will be dispatched once payment
is confirmed.
Invoice Forwarding: Forward the finalized invoice (prepared by the Finance Officer) to
the client.
VAT Bill Issuance: Issue the VAT bill to the client upon confirmation of payment and
product release, after necessary communication.
2. Invoice & Payment Management:
Payment Receipt: Receive and record client payments.
Payment Verification & Product Release: Verify payment receipt with the Finance
Officer and, upon confirmation, update the system to release products for dispatch.
Data Entry & Bookkeeping: Accurately record all financial transactions (e.g., payments,
expenses) into Tally software or other accounting systems.
3. Internal Coordination & Problem Resolution:
Financial Discrepancy Reporting: Immediately inform the Finance Officer about any
missing products, discrepancies in received goods, or issues that may lead to a refund.
Problem Coordination: Coordinate closely with the Finance Officer on resolving any
financial problems, payment issues, or refund processes.
Warehouse Liaison (Financial Aspect): Work with the Warehouse team to confirm
product receipt details relevant to invoicing and to ensure products are ready for
release post-payment.
4. Record Keeping & Compliance Support:
Financial Record Maintenance: Maintain organized and accessible records for all
invoices, payments, and related financial documents.
Basic Financial Support: Assist the Finance Officer with routine financial tasks and data
retrieval as required.

Invoice Forward Formate
Namaste Sir/Ma'am,
This is Rupesh from Rodhi Sources Account team.
Your invoice for the import cost is ready, as per our quotation. Please make the
payment for this invoice. Once paid, we will prepare your order for delivery. You will
receive a VAT bill with your products.
To pay your invoice, please let us know how you prefer:
Business Account: We will send you our business account details.
Personal Account: We will send you our personal account details. (Note: We do not
promote using personal accounts for business payments.)Before calling, verify all client details: amount, order number, invoice number,
and tracking information.
Begin the call by stating your name and company. Address clients as "Sir" or
"Ma'am. " Example: "Namaste, Sir/Ma'am, ma Rodhi warehouse bata Rupesh
boldaechu. "
Pay attention to the client's questions and responses to build a good
relationship.
Repeat important information back to the client to avoid any confusion.
End the call by explaining what will happen next. Example: "Ma hajur ko invoice
yahi number ko WhatsApp ma send garidinchu. "
If a client asks about delays or new products, politely explain that you will have
their CRM contact them. Be sure to then send a message to the "warehouse
team" group.
Maintain a calm and professional tone throughout the call, even with difficult
clients.
SUPPORT         |          SUCCESS          |           SATISFACTIONRoles and Responsibility of Warehouse Operation
Manager(WOM)/Warehouse Operation Assistant
Customs & Inbound Products
Customs Clearance: Go to the customs office to clear products. Monitor the process
to prevent damage or loss.
Safe Transport: Ensure all products are safely transported from customs to the
warehouse. 
Inventory Management
Receive & Count: Count all products upon arrival at the warehouse, using the
packing list. 
Shipping Label: Generate and attach a shipping label from Rodhi Sync to each
product.
Product Storage: Store all products safely and in an organized, easy-to-manage way.
Client & Dispatch Coordination
Client Communication: After the accountant releases a package, contact the client
to arrange dispatch.
Delivery Arrangement: Book ride-sharing apps like Pathao/Indrive for deliveries as
per client requests, and share the tracking link with the client.3PL Coordination: Request pickup from 3PL partners, provide the delivery
location, and send the tracking link to the client for tracking.
Delivery Confirmation: Follow up with the client after dispatch to confirm that
the product has been delivered.
Ready for Pickup: For clients picking up products, prepare the package and
place it in the designated holding area.
Team & Paperwork
Team Coordination: Work with the warehouse and head office teams to ensure
work is smooth and efficient.
Delivery Slips: Fill out a delivery slip for every product that leaves the
warehouse, and forward it to the head office team's front desk for the shipment
experiences survey.
Safety & Maintenance: Keep the warehouse clean and safe at all times. Properly
use and maintain all warehouse equipment.
Must follow these rules
Always wear ID card and dress provided by Company.
Treat every client's product as if it were your own. Handle all packages carefully
to prevent damage.
Take ownership of every task, from receiving to delivery. Your accuracy directly
impacts client satisfaction.
Use the "warehouse team" chat group for all important updates. Coordinate
with the accountant and head office to ensure everyone is on the same page.
Double-check every item against the packing list and shipping label before
dispatch. This prevents mistakes and wasted time.
If you are unsure about a task, a product's location, or a client's request, do not
guess. Ask the WOM or another team member for help.
Avoid rushing or being careless, as this can lead to damage. Report any
damaged items immediately.
All questions about delays, new products, or other issues should be redirected
to the client's CRM.
Do not use informal language or slang in professional communication, including
internal chat groups.
